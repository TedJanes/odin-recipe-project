Peanut Butter Jelly

Description

One of the most advanced Recipes handled by master chefs. Two slices of wheat bread with jelly and peanut butter in between. Couldn't ask for much else!

Ingredients

  • Wheat Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Take a knife and spread peanut butter on one side of one of the slices of bread
  2. Spread jelly with knife on one side of the other slice of bread
  3. Slap those bois together and enjoy
