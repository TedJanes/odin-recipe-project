Pork Tenderloin with Three Berry Salsa

Description

An egg from a chicken cracked and Scrambled. Baby birds are a nutritious source of B-Vitamins

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Put Butter in pan and crack an egg into it
  2. Start beating the egg vigourously till effectively scrambled.
  3. Add salt and pepper until no longer runny.
