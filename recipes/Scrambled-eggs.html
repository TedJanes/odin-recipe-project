Pork Tenderloin with Three Berry Salsa

Description

Very simple dish turned on its head with Berry suprise.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen blackberries
  • 1 cup frozen raspberries
  • 1 jalepeno pepper
  • 1 sweet red pepper
  • 3 tbsp cilantro
  • 2 pork tnederloins
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup chicken stalk

Steps

  1. Place the first 3 ingredients in a bowl; toss lightly to combine. Reserve 1 cup berry mixture for sauce. For salsa, gently stir onion, lime juice, cilantro and salt into remaining mixture; let stand 30 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, sprinkle pork with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon oil over medium-high heat. Add half the pork and cook until a thermometer inserted in pork reads 145Â°, 2-4 minutes on each side. Remove from pan. Repeat with remaining pork and oil.
  3. Add wine, shallots and reserved berry mixture to the pan, stirring to loosen browned bits. Bring to a boil; cook until liquid is reduced to 1 tablespoon, 4-6 minutes. Stir in stock; cook until shallots are tender, about 5 minutes longer, stirring occasionally. Return pork to pan; heat through. Serve with salsa.
